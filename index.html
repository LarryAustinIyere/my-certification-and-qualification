Ok, Where do I begin...

Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria. My dream was to be a Professional Medical Doctor but ended up gravitating towards Microbiology. Graduating with a First class degree honors in B.sc as a Microbiologist, I yearned to learn more, I yearned to learn something different. I’m a digital junkie. I love everything about the web. I remember my very first class on HTML. I just couldn’t get enough after that day. Front-end web development calls to all my passions; it incorporates creativity and problem solving and I’m allowed to break it and improve the code (in a separate Git branch, of course). I love applying responsive design principles and watching my web pages shrink into mobile screens and still look amazing. It’s oddly_satisfying:) . My specialty is mainly the ability to quickly learn new skills to improve my self and people around me. So far I have HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Git/GitHub, Node.JS, and Express.JS, Databases, Mongo DB, Mongoose, under my belt. I’ve started learning Mongo DB, Mongoose, etc. I’m still enthusiastically grabbing onto any other programming languages like Typescript, Dart, Python, etc.
